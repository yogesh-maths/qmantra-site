What is Percentage?

Percentage means “per hundred”. It is represented by the symbol %.

Formula

Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100

Example

If you score 45 marks out of 50:
Percentage = (45 ÷ 50) × 100 = 90%

Exam Tips

  • Always convert fractions to percentage
  • Use shortcuts for 10%, 25%, 50%
