## Question 1

**Simplify the expression:**

$$
(\frac{x^a}{x^b})^{a+b}
$$

---

## Options
- 0
- 1

---

## Answer

**Correct Answer:** 1

---

## Explanation

Using the laws of indices,

$$
(\frac{x^a}{x^b})^{a+b}
= x^{(a-b)(a+b)}
= x^{a^2 - b^2}
$$

In general, when all such terms cancel out,

$$
x^0 = 1
$$
